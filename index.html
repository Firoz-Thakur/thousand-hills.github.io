Shata Dhar ORG ( Tourism Talks )

Shata Dhar Organization (@shatadharvalley) is a passionate team of individuals that spans across various locations in India, including Himachal Pradesh, Bangalore, Chennai, Lucknow, Pune, Mumbai and beyond. Our primary objective is to promote unexplored tourism places and highlight the rich history of deities and religious sites not only in Himachal Pradesh but other states as well.
